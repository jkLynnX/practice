## Hobby Loss Protection Checklist

Document these items to defend your farm as a legitimate business:

- [ ] Written business plan exists (what you're growing, timeline to profitability, target market)
- [ ] Records of species planted and growth progress
- [ ] Texas ag exemption approval on file
- [ ] Evidence of time spent managing the farm (calendar entries, photos, notes)
- [ ] Market research for tree sales (Christmas trees, timber, nursery stock)
- [ ] Separate bank account for farm finances (or at minimum, clearly tracked expenses)
- [ ] Consultation with agricultural extension service or forestry experts
- [ ] Business cards, website, or other marketing materials (if any)

> The more boxes you check, the stronger your position if the IRS questions your profit motive.
