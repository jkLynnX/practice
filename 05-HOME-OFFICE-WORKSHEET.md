## Tips for Gathering These Numbers

- **Mortgage interest & property tax:** Form 1098 from your mortgage company
- **Electricity:** Add up all 12 monthly bills, or check your electric provider's website for annual usage summary
- **Gas/heating:** Same approach — add up 12 months
- **Water:** Same approach
- **Internet:** Monthly bill x 12
- **Insurance:** Annual premium from your declarations page
- **Home repairs:** Gather receipts for any whole-house repairs (HVAC service, plumbing, roof repair, etc.)
- **Home purchase price:** From your closing documents or county records
- **Land value:** From your property tax assessment (it separates land from improvements)
